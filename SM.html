Service Management

The Service Management track is centered around managing and delivering IT services that meet business needs and ensure optimal customer satisfaction. Students learn about IT service management frameworks such as ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library), along with topics in service delivery, support, and operational processes. The track emphasizes skills in project management, service quality assurance, and customer relationship management. Graduates are trained to handle roles such as IT service managers, support specialists, and IT consultants, focusing on optimizing service delivery and managing the full lifecycle of IT services within organizations.
