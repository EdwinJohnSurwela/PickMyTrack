Network Technology

This track focuses on the design, implementation, and management of computer networks. Students learn about network infrastructure, including routers, switches, and servers, and how to configure and maintain them. The curriculum also covers topics like network security, troubleshooting, wireless technologies, and network protocols. Graduates of this track are equipped with the skills needed to work as network administrators, system analysts, or network engineers, ensuring businesses have secure, efficient, and reliable communication systems.
