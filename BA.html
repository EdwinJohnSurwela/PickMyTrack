Business Analytics

The Business Analytics track involves using data to analyze trends, make informed decisions, and support business strategy. Students learn techniques in data mining, statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and data visualization, helping organizations transform raw data into actionable insights. This track focuses on business intelligence tools and software to analyze and interpret data, as well as communicate findings to support decision-making. Graduates are prepared for roles such as data analysts, business intelligence specialists, or data scientists, helping organizations leverage their data to drive growth and improve performance
